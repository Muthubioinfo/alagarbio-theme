About us

We are a private Limited company engaged in selling high-quality Horticultural products since 1999. Our Horticultural products majorly includes a wide range of indoor plants for your lovely home and workspace, and outdoor flowering and fruit plants, Medicinal plants, Shrubs, Avenue trees. We also majorly sell durable and high-quality polypropylene (PP) pot containers in different sizes. In addition, we sell our own products of nutrient-rich manure such as Ready-mix soil, growth media and natural fertilisers to ensure enrichment, sustainability, and disease-resistance for healthy plant growth. At present, we have all types of Indoor plants for your lovely home and workspace, and outdoor flowering and fruit varieties, Medicinal plants, Shrubs, Bonsai plants and avenue trees.
